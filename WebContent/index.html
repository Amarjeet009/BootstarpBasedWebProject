About Us

NIIT Technologies is a global IT solutions organization addressing the requirements of clients across the Americas, Europe, Asia, and Australia. NIIT Technologies services clients in travel and transportation, banking and financial services, insurance, manufacturing, and media verticals, offering a range of services, including Application Development and Maintenance, Infrastructure Management, and Business Process Management. Focused on Digital Services, the Company is helping businesses design agile, scalable, and digital operating models. NIIT Technologies has been assessed at leading global quality benchmarks and standards including the ISO 9001:2000, ISO: 27001 (an Information Security Management accreditation), Level 5 of SEI CMMi version 1.2, COPC, PCMM level 5, and the international ISO:20000 (IT management standard).

RAJENDRA S PAWAR

Chairman & Managing Director

Mr. Rajendra S Pawar, Chairman and Co-Founder of NIIT Group that encompasses NIIT Limited, a leading Global Talent Development Corporation, and NIIT Technologies Limited, the software and services arm. As a co-founder of the NIIT Group, Mr. Pawar has not only revolutionized the IT training industry, but is also recognized as a global technology thought leader. As the Chairman of India’s IT industry body- National Association of Software and Service Companies (NASSCOM), Mr. Pawar has led several ICT industry initiatives, giving voice to the sector’s aspirations and goals. A Fellow member of the Computer Society of India (CSI) and the Institution of Electronic and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE), Mr. Pawar was awarded an Honorary Doctoral Degree by Rajiv Gandhi Technical University (MP) in 2005.

S. VISWANATHAN

Chief People Officer

S. Viswanathan is the HR head at NIIT Technologies and has been associated with the organization since it was established in 2004. Viswanathan have experience of 25 years, much of it in different roles within various businesses in the NIIT group. In his last role he was heading Strategic Alliances and has played a pivotal role in all the mergers and acquisitions, most recent being the strategic investment in Incessant Technologies for building the Digital Integration capabilities of NIIT Technologies. Earlier he has worked as the Chief Operating Officer of NIIT SmartServe. He holds a master degree in Business Management from XLRI, Jamshedpur and Applied Mathematics from Anna University, Chennai. Viswanathan is married and has a daughter. As a family they enjoy travelling.

ARVIND THAKUR

Chief Executive Officer and Joint Managing Director

Mr Arvind Thakur is the CEO and Joint Managing Director of NIIT Technologies Ltd, a global IT solutions and business services organization.In 2012 he was conferred the prestigious global Gold Stevie award as Executive of the Year in the computer services category. He serves on the boards of the company’s overseas subsidiaries and is the Chairman of the domestic joint venture with ESRI Inc. He is also a director on an innovative venture, Hole-in-the Wall Education Ltd, enabling technology to be deployed for educating the masses, and serves on the Board of Management of NIIT University, a not for profit institution. Mr Thakur graduated in engineering from IIT- Kharagpur where he received the Institute Silver medal for ranking first in the department and started his career with BHEL, before joining NIIT in 1985.

HOLLY J. MORRIS

Non-Executive Independent Director

Dr. Holly J. Morris has extensive experience as a corporate IT and operations executive in Fortune 500 global financial services organizations, local government and manufacturing. From 2006 to 2012, she was the CIO at Thrivent Financial.Holly also served as the SVP of technologies at American Express Global Financial Services. Holly has been advising global IT delivery companies and has continued several volunteer activities, serving universities, non-profits and government on innovative ways to re-invent themselves given today’s technology opportunities and challenges. Holly holds a BA in Classics from Kent State University, an MA and PhD in Ancient Studies from the University of Minnesota and is close to completing her masters in software systems from the University of Saint Thomas in Saint Paul, MN.
